Be the Leader in Creating a Lifetime of Exciting and Memorable Experiences

Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America is located about 30 minutes south of Atlanta in Newnan, Georgia. We employ 1600 people in our 1,355,000 square foot facility, which consists of four plants and two distribution centers. We manufacture 4 recreational product lines including Golf Cars, All Terrain Vehicles (ATV's), Personal Watercraft and Recreational Off-road Vehicles (ROV's). We do not sell directly to the public from our facility.

Our products are sold and distributed in the U.S. by Yamaha Motor Corporation USA, which is headquartered in California.

Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation is an equal opportunity employer. We are always looking for new talent to join our team. We encourage you to check out the Career Opportunities listed on this site.
