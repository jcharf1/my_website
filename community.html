Corporate Social Responsibility

YMMC Integrated Management System Policy

The future is in our hands. For the sustainability and success of our company, all Yamaha employees, supervisors, managers executives and representatives must:

  • Take action to meet or exceed requirements and regulations
  • Protect the health and safety of employees by preventing work related injuries, ill health, diseases and incidents
  • Make and design products that impress our customers
  • Reduce and manage identified hazards and risks
  • Realize success by creating and achieving goals
  • Promote quality and the protection of the environment, including prevention of pollution

Plan, Do, Act is our process for continual improvement

Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) has a long-standing commitment of giving back to our surrounding communities and supporting the Yamaha Motor Corporation Global Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) goals.

All Corporate Donations are funded by the YMMC Recycling program where our employees recycle waste materials such as plastic, cardboard and metal, allowing YMMC to reduce our impact on the environment and support community efforts and organizations

The YMMC Corporate Donation Program includes:

  • Employee Donation Matching
  • Employee Volunteer Matching
  • Corporate Donations to our local Target communities

YMMC Corporate Giving Philosophy
While support in other areas may be considered, our corporate giving philosophy focuses its support towards qualified 501(c)(3) or state/local government 170(c)(1) organizations which provide services in the following focus areas:

  • Developing Future Leaders through promoting youth activities, leadership, character development and education, with a particular focus in STEM education (science, technology, engineering and math)
  • Local Emergency Support Services including fire, rescue and law enforcement
  • Environment conservation, improvement, or biodiversity
  • Safety in the workplace, home or outdoors
  • Basic Human Needs including food, clothing, shelter and medical care/support to help develop economic self-reliance and create pipelines to employment

YMMC uses GivingTrax to manage donation requests and communicate additional giving guidelines. If you are a non-profit and not yet registered with GivingTrax, it’s free and easy sign up: GivingTrax Registration

Once registered you can submit a donation request by visiting the YMMC Community Contribution GivingTrax page.

YMMC employees may submit personal donations to be matched at the YMMC Employee Match GivingTrax page.
